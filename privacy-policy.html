Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the online services offered by KaushalSiksha at and its subdomains. These services are collectively referred to as the "Services".

Overview

We respect your privacy and strive to provide a safe and secure user experience. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Services.

By using KaushalSiksha, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Your data may be stored and processed on servers located within or outside India. By using our Services, you consent to the transfer of your data outside India if required.

Our Services may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or policies of those third-party websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing your personal information.

Collection of Information

Personal Information Provided by You

In certain areas of our Services, we may request you to provide personal information including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Educational details
  • Internship preferences
  • Resume information
  • Organization details (for employers)

This information may be collected when you register for internships, fill out forms, contact us, or apply for internship opportunities.

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect certain information including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses

This information helps us analyze user behavior and improve the functionality of our website.

Information Collected Through Third-Party Services

KaushalSiksha may integrate with third-party services such as Google, analytics providers, or social media platforms. If you choose to sign in using third-party authentication services such as Google Sign-In, we may receive basic information including your name and email address.

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Information About Your Contacts

If you choose to share internship opportunities or website content with your contacts through social media or messaging features, we may process the contact information required to send the shared content.

Information Collected Through Use of Services

We may collect information about how you interact with our platform including:

This information helps us provide better services and relevant opportunities to users.

We retain user information for as long as necessary to provide our services and improve user experience. Since career development is a long-term process, certain information may be retained for future use unless you request deletion of your account.

If you apply for internships through KaushalSiksha, your profile information may be shared with the organizations offering those internships.
